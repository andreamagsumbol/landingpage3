    Alycia Debnam-Carey

    Alycia Jasmin Debnam-Carey is an Australian actress. She is best known for her portrayals of the characters Lexa on the dystopian science fiction television series The 100 and Alicia Clark on the AMC horror drama series Fear the Walking Dead.

    Dylan O'brien

    Dylan Rhodes O'Brien is an American actor. His first major role was Stiles Stilinski on the MTV supernatural drama Teen Wolf, where he was a series regular during all six seasons.

    Aron Piper

    Arón Julio Manuel Piper Barbero is a German-Spanish actor, singer and model known for playing Ander Muñoz in the Netflix teen drama Elite.

    Úrsula Corberó

    Úrsula Corberó Delgado is a Spanish actress and model. She is best known for her roles as Ruth Gómez in Física o química, Margarita de Austria in the television series Isabel and Marta in the comedy film Cómo Sobrevivir a una Despedida.

    Emilia Clarke

    Emilia Isobel Euphemia Rose Clarke is an English actress. She studied at the Drama Centre London, appearing in a number of stage productions. Her television debut was a guest appearance in the BBC One medical soap opera Doctors in 2009.

    Viola Davis

    Viola Davis is an American actress and producer. The recipient of various accolades, including an Academy Award, a Primetime Emmy Award, and two Tony Awards, she is the first African-American to achieve the Triple Crown of Acting.
